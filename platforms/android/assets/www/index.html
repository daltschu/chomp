Chomp

This game has two players alternately removing (eating) squares from a chocolate tablet. To play, touch any square and this will remove all the squares which are to the right and above it.

Here is an example: if you choose the square on the third line and the second column from the top right corner, you remove the six squares which are on the blue line or to the right or above it. The chosen square is at the corner of the line.

The yellow square at the bottom left is poisonous, so whichever player has to eat it loses. You play first and the app plays second.
